About Hardrock

Hard Rock Pavers is a privately owned hydraulic high pressure concrete products manufactures in Kottayam. Stylish and strong, our pavers and blocks are superior to conventional concrete products in appearance and performance. Our products have an uncompromising strength, superior durability and outstanding quality.

Our fully automated manufacturing process uses a German technology press utilizing tons of pressure to eliminate excess water and air, and precision moulds to ensure uniform size and shape.

Quality Management

All our products are crafted to the highest quality with elegance and durability in mind. All our process right from raw material procurement to shipping the finished goods to customers are monitored. All the product batches undergo a random compressive strength test and the quality is ensured before shipping.The benefits of our products guarantee quality for many years to come.
